# Plan Comparison

MergeGuide offers five plans. Tier gating is enforced server-side — the features available to your organization are determined by your active subscription.

## Overage Behavior

When you exceed your monthly evaluation limit:

| Plan | Behavior |
|------|---------|
| Free | Hard block — evaluations stop until next month |
| Pro | Soft warning — evaluations continue, notification sent |
| Team | 10% grace period — evaluations continue up to 110% of limit |
| Business | 10% grace period |
| Enterprise | Unlimited — never triggers |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I try Business features before upgrading?**

New accounts start on a Trial plan that includes Pro features plus OSCAL export, so you can evaluate the export workflow. Trial does not include SAML, SCIM, or custom roles — contact sales to arrange an extended trial of Business or Enterprise features.

**What happens when I exceed my seat limit?**

New invitations are blocked. Existing members retain access. Add more seats from **Settings > Billing > Seats** to invite additional members.

**Can I mix monthly and annual seats?**

No. All seats in an organization are on the same billing cycle.

**Is there a nonprofit or startup discount?**

Contact [sales@mergeguide.ai](mailto:sales@mergeguide.ai) for details.
