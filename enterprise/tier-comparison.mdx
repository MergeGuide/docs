# Plan Comparison

MergeGuide offers five plans. Tier gating is enforced server-side — the features available to your organization are determined by your active subscription.

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Seat Minimum | Billing |
|------|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Free** | $0 | — | No billing required |
| **Pro** | $29/seat/month | — | Monthly or annual (~18% off) |
| **Team** | $39/seat/month | 5 seats | Monthly or annual (~18% off) |
| **Business** | $79/seat/month | 10 seats | Monthly or annual (~18% off) |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | — | Annual pricing, contact sales |

Annual billing is available for all paid plans with approximately 18% discount. Enterprise pricing is custom — contact [sales@mergeguide.ai](mailto:sales@mergeguide.ai).

## Overage Behavior

When you exceed your monthly evaluation limit:

| Plan | Behavior |
|------|---------|
| Free | Hard block — evaluations stop until next month |
| Pro | Soft warning — evaluations continue, notification sent |
| Team | 10% grace period — evaluations continue up to 110% of limit |
| Business | 10% grace period |
| Enterprise | Unlimited — never triggers |

## Compliance Framework Coverage

Framework access is not tier-gated — all plans with compliance reports enabled (Team+) can access all 18 frameworks. The difference is in OSCAL export (Business+) and evidence export (Pro+).

| Framework | Available On |
|-----------|-------------|
| 18 compliance frameworks (NIST, SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001, etc.) | Team, Business, Enterprise |
| OSCAL export | Business, Enterprise |
| Evidence export (CSV/JSON) | Pro, Team, Business, Enterprise |

See [Dashboard](/user-guides/dashboard) for the full framework list.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I try Business features before upgrading?**

New accounts start on a Trial plan that includes Pro features plus OSCAL export, so you can evaluate the export workflow. Trial does not include SAML, SCIM, or custom roles — contact sales to arrange an extended trial of Business or Enterprise features.

**What happens when I exceed my seat limit?**

New invitations are blocked. Existing members retain access. Add more seats from **Settings > Billing > Seats** to invite additional members.

**Can I mix monthly and annual seats?**

No. All seats in an organization are on the same billing cycle.

**Is there a nonprofit or startup discount?**

Contact sales. We offer discounted pricing for qualified startups and nonprofits.
