# Team Management

Manage your team at **Settings > Team**. Only Owners and Admins can invite members or modify roles.

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access — delete org, configure SSO/SCIM, all Admin actions |
| **Admin** | Manage policies, invite/remove members, configure SCM connections, all Developer actions |
| **Developer** | View evaluations, manage personal API keys |
| **Viewer** | View evaluations and compliance data, no write access |

Each organization has exactly one Owner (the account that created it). Ownership can be transferred from **Settings > Team > Transfer Ownership**.

## Inviting Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team settings">
    Go to **Settings > Team > Invite Member**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email and role">
    Enter the member's email address and select their role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invitation">
    Click **Send Invite**. The member receives an email with a link to accept the invitation.

    Invitations expire after 7 days. Resend from **Settings > Team > Pending Invitations**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When the invited member accepts:
- If they do not have a MergeGuide account, they are prompted to create one
- If they already have an account, they are added to your organization and can switch to it from the org switcher

<Note>
Inviting a member consumes a seat. If you are at your seat limit, contact your account administrator or [support@mergeguide.ai](mailto:support@mergeguide.ai).
</Note>

## Role Permissions

| Action | Viewer | Developer | Admin | Owner |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|-------|
| View evaluations | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View compliance reports | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Export compliance evidence | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Manage personal API keys | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Enable/disable policies | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Configure SCM connections | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Invite members | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Remove members | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Change member roles | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Configure SSO/SCIM | No | No | No | Yes |
| Delete organization | No | No | No | Yes |

## Changing a Member's Role

1. Go to **Settings > Team**
2. Find the member and click **Edit**
3. Select the new role
4. Save

Role changes take effect immediately on the member's next page load.

## Removing Members

1. Go to **Settings > Team**
2. Find the member and click **Remove**
3. Confirm removal

Removed members lose access immediately. Their data (evaluations attributed to them, API keys created by them) is retained. The seat is freed.

If the organization uses SSO enforcement, the member is also prevented from signing in via SSO on their next attempt.

## Seat Limits by Plan

| Plan | Maximum Seats |
|------|--------------|
| Free | 3 |
| Pro | 5 |
| Team | 25 |
| Business | 50 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

When you reach the seat limit, invitations are disabled until you add more seats or remove existing members.

## Pending Invitations

View all pending invitations at **Settings > Team > Pending Invitations**.

Actions available:
- **Resend** — sends the invitation email again with a fresh 7-day expiry
- **Cancel** — invalidates the invitation link

## Organization Switching

Users who are members of multiple MergeGuide organizations can switch between them without signing out.

To switch organizations:
1. Click your organization name in the top-left of the dashboard
2. Select a different organization from the dropdown
3. The dashboard reloads in the context of the selected organization

Each organization has its own policies, repositories, evaluations, and settings. Switching does not affect the other organization's state.

## SCIM Auto-Provisioning

SCIM v2 is available for automatic user lifecycle management. When SCIM is configured, your identity provider manages MergeGuide membership automatically — manual invitation is not required.

See [SCIM Provisioning](/enterprise/scim-provisioning).

## Audit Log

All team management actions are recorded in the audit log: invitations sent, roles changed, members removed, SSO and SCIM configuration changes.

View the audit log at **Settings > Security > Audit Log**. Audit log retention varies by plan:

| Plan | Audit Log Retention |
|------|---------------------|
| Free | 7 days |
| Pro | 30 days |
| Team | 60 days |
| Business | 90 days |
| Enterprise | 2 years |

Export the audit log as CSV or JSON from **Settings > Security > Audit Log > Export**.
